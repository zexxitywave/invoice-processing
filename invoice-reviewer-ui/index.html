What happens after upload?

1️⃣  PDF lands in S3 — EventBridge detects the new file
2️⃣  Step Functions orchestrates the pipeline
3️⃣  Textract extracts vendor, date, totals & confidence scores
4️⃣  Bedrock AI validates and assigns risk level
5️⃣  DynamoDB stores the result
6️⃣  If confidence < 95% → SES email sent for human review
Results appear on the Dashboard within ~30 seconds.
